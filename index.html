Elfs vs Zobbies

Play Game

Introduction

Long ago, Elfes lived together in harmony. Then everything changed when Zombies attacked their lands. These zombies are different from other zombies. They are very evil and tricky. Only you, the master of the ancient knowledge, can stop them. You are the last hope for the Elves. Their life depends on you. They believe you can save their world.

Game Rules

Only the smartest one can win this battle. There are four topics in this game. Every topic contains four types of questions. You can choose two types of spell either healing or attack. If you answer correctly the spell is applied. After this move to pass the enemy.

Good luck in the battle!
